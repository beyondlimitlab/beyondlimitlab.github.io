Lovel – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-25

Overview

Lovel is an emotional letter app. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

  • Account identifiers (e.g., Firebase user ID) to provide login and account features.
  • User content (letters, text blocks, and optional images you upload) to deliver app functionality.
  • Usage/diagnostics (limited technical logs if available on your device/platform) to maintain and improve stability.

How We Use Data

  • Provide core features (create, send, view letters).
  • Maintain account and security.
  • Improve reliability and fix bugs.

Third-Party Services

Lovel uses Firebase services (Authentication, Firestore, and Storage) to store and deliver app data.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account in the app. When you request deletion, we delete your account record and associated account data. Content you have sent to other users may remain visible to those recipients.
